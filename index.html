📖 Game Guide

🚀 Goal

Become the biggest Space Cafe in the galaxy! Serve customers, earn credits, unlock new items and prestige to gain permanent power-ups.

💰 How to Earn

  • Customer ships arrive on the left with an order bubble above them.
  • Click a workstation (bottom) to start making that item.
  • When it glows & pulses → it's ready!
  • Drag the glowing workstation onto the matching ship to earn credits.
  • On mobile: tap the workstation, then tap the customer ship.

🤖 Automation

  • Station Manager — auto-starts production when a customer orders.
  • Serving Droid — auto-delivers finished products to the right ship.

⚡ Space Events

  • ☄️ Meteor Shower — all customers lose patience faster! Serve quickly.
  • 👑 VIP Customer — a special ship arrives worth 5x income!
  • 🌊 Galactic Rush — double customer arrival rate for 30 seconds.

🔮 Prestige

Reset the game to earn Dark Matter. Spend Dark Matter on permanent upgrades that carry over every run!

🎁 Daily Bonus

Return each day to claim free Dark Matter. Bonus increases with each prestige!
